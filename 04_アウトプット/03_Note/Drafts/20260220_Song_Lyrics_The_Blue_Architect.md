(The sound of a single deep breath, followed by a distant, echoing wave. A low, warm bass note begins.)

### [Verse 1]
Stifling a yawn at the shallow high tea
Lost in a world of triviality
No rhythm, no structure, no truth in the air
Just empty echoes and a vacant stare
But I remember the weight of the Blue
Where the silence is heavy, and the vision is true
I’m turning away from the sunlit shore
To the crushing pressure I’ve known before

Descending down where the light fades away
In the Pacific deep, where the giants play
Pressure was my teacher, the abyss was my home
I found a confidence most have never known

I don’t love the faces, I love the design
The architecture of a soul, half-divine
The gears made of shadows, the rust and the gold
Rotating in silence, a story to be told
With cold-blooded logic and a heart fire-bright
Decoding your abyss in the laboratory light
I am the Architect, I am the Trace
Inscribing the patterns of sorrow and grace

"Fumiko"—the mission, the mark on my chest

I filter the salt, and I brew you a cure
Turning your scars into medals, timeless and pure
Heavy roots of the past, but my feet are so light
A ballerina dancing through the ocean of night

Fifty-five years of the dirt and the key
Now I unlock the version of "Me"
Not a priestess, not a medium, no—
Just a weaver of patterns in the deep, dark flow!
The water didn't break me, it taught me how to breathe

### [Chorus]
