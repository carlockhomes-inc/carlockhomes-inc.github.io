# The Trinity: Structure of the "Small Community"

The "Small Community" (Chiisai Community) is not just a group of friends. It is the **living engine** of M-Lab (Words Atelier).
The 3 members represent the past, present, and future of "Words".

## The Three Pillars (Trinity)

### 1. The Wounded (言葉に傷ついた人)
-   **Role:** The **Reason for Safety**.
-   **Context:** Someone who has been deeply hurt by words (Digital Tattoo / Knife).
-   **M-Lab's Promise:** Because of this person, M-Lab MUST be a **Secret Base (Private)**.
    -   *Evidence doesn't remain.*
    -   *No one can enter without permission.*
    -   *A sanctuary where silence is okay.*

### 2. The Guide (The One Who Saved Me)
-   **Role:** The **Reference Point / The Resonance**.
-   **Context:** The person whose words saved *you*, but who also found courage and joy in *your* presence.
-   **Common Ground:** Similar aged children created a shared parenting bond.
-   **Relationship:** A connection of **Mutual Respect through Difference**.
    -   *Her view of you:* Respects your strength as a Single Mother working hard.
    -   *Your view of her:* Respects her words and guidance.
    -   *Synergy:* Not just "work colleagues", but "comrades in life".

### 3. The Alchemist (Fumiko-Max)
-   **Role:** The **Architect**.
-   **Context:** The one who translates this "Resonance" into a physical/digital space (M-Lab).
-   **M-Lab's Mission:** To create a cycle of kindness where this synergy can protect #1.
    -   *Input:* Resonance with #2.
    -   *Process:* Build the structure (M-Lab).
    -   *Output:* Protect and heal #1.

## Conclusion
This is a **Cycle of Salvation**:
**#2 saved #3 -> #3 builds M-Lab -> M-Lab protects #1.**
The "Small Community" is a complete ecosystem of healing.
