# 🎨 M-Lab Logo Design Prompts (Deep Sea & Axis)

Server capacity is currently limiting image generation, but here are the detailed "recipes" (prompts) for the M-Lab logos. We can use these later or for a human designer.

## 🧪 Concept A: Minimal Science (Axis & Flow)
**Concept**: "Structuring Chaos into a Beautiful Axis"
**Prompt**:
> A minimalist, high-end logo for 'M-Lab'.
> **Symbol**: A stylized laboratory flask drawn with a single, elegant silver line.
> **Core**: Inside the flask, there is a perfectly straight, glowing vertical line (The Axis) in cyan/white.
> **Flow**: Around the axis, a subtle, smoke-like blue spiral (DNA-like structure) wraps gently, representing the restructuring of thoughts.
> **Background**: Deep Indigo Blue (Matte finish).
> **Vibe**: Intellectual, Scientific, Expensive, Calm.
> **Text**: "M-Lab" in a thin, modern sans-serif font below.

## 🌊 Concept B: The Deep Dive (Typography Core)
**Concept**: "The Mariana Trench of Thought"
**Prompt**:
> A typographic logo for 'M-Lab'.
> **Lettering**: The letter 'M' is the masterpiece. The 'V' shape in the middle of the 'M' extends deep downwards, fading into a dark gradient like a deep sea trench.
> **Effect**: A beam of light shoots down from the top center through the trench, illuminating the depths.
> **Ripples**: Faint, concentric ripples expand horizontally from this light beam.
> **Colors**: Dark Navy, Bioluminescent Blue, Silver.
> **Style**: Abstract, Cinematic, Futuristic.

## ✒️ Concept C: The Drop (Writer's Soul)
**Concept**: "One Drop Changes Everything"
**Prompt**:
> A macro, realistic 3D render logo.
> **Action**: A single drop of black ink is hitting a perfectly still, dark blue water surface.
> **Shape**: The falling drop is morphed into the shape of a classic gold/silver fountain pen nib.
> **Impact**: The ring ripple on the water is perfect and sharp.
> **Lighting**: Rim lighting highlights the metallic texture of the nib-drop.
> **Meaning**: The moment thought becomes text.
> **Text**: "M-Lab" in a classic serif font (like Times New Roman but modern).

## 🧭 Concept D: The Compass (Explorer)
**Concept**: "Navigating the Sea of Thoughts"
**Prompt**:
> A geometric logo combining a Compass and a Pen.
> **Symbol**: A compass needle (N/S) where the North point is a pen nib and the South point is a flask bottom.
> **Background**: A faint star map or grid lines (blueprint style) in the background.
> **Color**: Deep Ocean Blue and Brass/Gold.
> **Vibe**: Exploration, Architecture, Guidance.

## 🚀 Concept E: Anti-Gravity (Ascending Spiral)
**Concept**: "Defying Gravity with Thought"
**Inspiration**: Inspired by the acrobatics that ignore gravity.
**Prompt**:
> A futuristic, scientific logo for 'M-Lab'.
> **Symbol**: A modern glass flask. Inside, a liquid (glowing cyan/neon green) is not settling at the bottom but spiraling upwards in a double helix against gravity.
> **Energy**: Dynamic, upward motion, potential energy released.
> **Background**: Matte Black.
> **Vibe**: Innovative, Powerful, "Immortals" theme.

## 🤸‍♀️ Concept F: The Leap (Dynamic Arc)
**Concept**: "The Beautiful Trajectory of a Jump"
**Inspiration**: The perfect form of an acrobat mid-air.
**Prompt**:
> A typographic logo where the letter 'M' is dynamic.
> **Shape**: The strokes of the 'M' are curved like the trajectory of a jump or a diver. It looks like it's frozen in mid-air.
> **Line**: Clean, sharp lines like a dancer's limb.
> **Accent**: A small spark or star at the highest point of the curve (the apex).
> **Color**: Silver and Electric Blue.
> **Text**: "M-Lab" integrated into the motion.
